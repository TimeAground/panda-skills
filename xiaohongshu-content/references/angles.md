# Content Angle Templates

## Angle 1: Tool Recommendation (Discovery)

Best for: new tools, trending products, hidden gems

Hook formula: "I found a [adjective] tool that can [benefit]"

Structure:
- Hook: surprising fact or pain point
- What it does (3 bullet points max)
- Who needs it (3 scenarios)
- How to get it
- CTA: comment for resource pack

## Angle 2: Story-driven (Builder Story)

Best for: indie dev stories, origin stories, underdog narratives

Hook formula: "After [dramatic event], he/she built [product]"

Structure:
- Story hook (the drama)
- What they built
- Key data points (stars, users, revenue)
- Lesson or insight
- CTA: what do you think?

## Angle 3: Comparison (VS)

Best for: alternatives, free vs paid, new vs old

Hook formula: "[Free tool] vs [Paid tool], the result surprised me"

Structure:
- Setup the comparison
- Side by side (3-5 dimensions)
- Verdict
- CTA: which one do you prefer?

## Angle 4: Tutorial (How-to)

Best for: tools with visual output, step-by-step processes

Hook formula: "3 steps to [achieve result] for free"

Structure:
- Show the end result first
- Step by step (3-5 steps, simplified)
- Tips and tricks
- CTA: comment for detailed guide
